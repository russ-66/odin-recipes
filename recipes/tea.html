Tea!

Description

Perfect hot tea to warm you up on a cold winter day!!!

Ingredients

  • 1x Tea bag (flavour of your choosing)
  • Hot water (a reasonable amount)
  • Sugar (optional and based on your taste!)
  • Your favourite cup!

Instructions

  1. Fill kettle with reasonable amount of water then boil it
  2. Whilst waiting for kettle to boil, put tea bag in your favourite cup!
  3. Wait for kettle to boil
  4. After kettle has boiled, fill cup with tea bag in it with water
  5. Wait 2 minutes or however long as suggested for tea to brew
  6. Once tea has brewed, enjoy!
