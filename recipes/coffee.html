Coffee!

Description

Aeropressto coffee!!!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz of coffee beans (flavour of your choosing)
  • Hot water (a reasonable amount)
  • Sugar (optional and based on your taste!)
  • Your favourite cup!

Instructions

  1. Fill kettle with reasonable amount of water then boil it
  2. Whilst waiting for kettle to boil, grind coffee beans to medium grind or your choosing
  3. Wait for kettle to boil
  4. Put coffee in aeropress using the reverse method and fill water 1/4 of the way
  5. Stir for 30 seconds, get cup ready!
  6. Aeropress the coffee into the cup, enjoy!
